Privacy Policy

Adobe Stock Review Helper

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or user information. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected (name, email, address, etc.)
  • No browsing history or activity is tracked
  • No data is sent to any external server
  • No analytics or tracking scripts are used
  • No cookies are set by the extension

Local Storage

The extension stores two preferences locally on your device using your browser's localStorage:

  • Enable/disable toggle state — whether the extension panel is active
  • PRO activation status — your PayPal order ID to remember your purchase

This data never leaves your browser and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Website Access

The extension only runs on contributor.stock.adobe.com review pages. It reads page content solely to classify your uploaded files by type (Commercial, Editorial, AI-generated) and display visual highlights. No data from the page is collected or transmitted.

In-App Purchases

The optional PRO upgrade is processed entirely through PayPal. We do not have access to your payment details. PayPal's own privacy policy governs the payment transaction.

Third-Party Services

The extension itself makes no network requests. The optional payment page uses PayPal's checkout service, which is subject to PayPal's Privacy Policy.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the new version will be posted at this URL.

Contact
